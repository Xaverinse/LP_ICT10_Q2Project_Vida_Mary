Welcome to Night Raven College

Night Raven College has a history of over 500 years and was founded by humans.

About Our School

Night Raven College is an all-boys academy. It is a four-year magic educational institution located on top of a mountain on the north side of the Isle of Sages.

Students begin the school year in September every year. Assuming that the student fulfills the requirements to advance to the next school year each time, they will spend the first three years studying in Night Raven College before beginning their internships in their fourth year. Students are required to take certain mandatory subjects each year, and are also able to read elective subjects to their preferences.

Night Raven College's academic calendar is divided into two semesters, each ending with a final exam before the summer holidays and winter holidays begin. When the winter holidays begin, students are given special permission to return home via the Mirror Chamber, where they have to go through the correct mirror that will send them to their desired destination.

Night Raven College's campus is located at the north of Sage's Island on top of a mountain with the main building located on an elevated piece of land. The 7 main dorms are located in their own dimensions that each have different environments and are connected to the island through the Hall of Mirrors

Each of the seven dorms at Night Raven College is modeled after The Great Seven, a group of individuals legendary in Twisted Wonderland. Upon entering the school, students are sorted into these dorms based on the qualities of their souls.

Dorms/Clubs include:

Diasomnia, Pomefiore, Octavinelle, Scarabia, Ignihyde, Heartslabyul, and Savanaclaw

Night Raven College has in its faculty a selection of talented professors each specialised in a specific department. There are a variety of classes these included both the core curriculum and the electives, available from first year forward.

Classes include:

History of Magic, Flying, Alchemy, Potionology, Physical Education, Magic Analysis, Poison Refining, Astrology, Practical Magic, Art, Musicology, Animal Linguistics and Ancient Magic
